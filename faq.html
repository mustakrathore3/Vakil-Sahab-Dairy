Frequently Asked Questions

What is Vakil Sahab Dairy?
Vakil Sahab Dairy is a comprehensive legal case management system designed to help advocates manage their cases, track hearings, and maintain client records efficiently.
How do I register as an Advocate?
Click on the "Register" button on the home page and fill in your details including name, date of birth, chamber address, mobile number, email, and create a secure password.
Can I add multiple operators?
Yes! As an admin, you can create multiple operator accounts from the Admin Dashboard. Go to "Operators" section and click "Add New Operator".
How do clients access their cases?
Clients can log in using their mobile number registered with their case. They will receive an OTP for secure login and can view all their case details and hearing dates.
Can I track hearing dates?
Yes! The system provides a comprehensive hearing calendar where you can track upcoming hearings, set reminders, and never miss an important date.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely! We use Firebase's secure authentication and database services. All data is encrypted and stored securely in the cloud.
What if I forget my password?
On the login page, click "Forgot Password" and follow the instructions. You will receive a password reset email to your registered email address.
Can I generate reports?
Yes! The Admin Dashboard provides comprehensive reporting features where you can view case statistics, pending hearings, and generate custom reports.
